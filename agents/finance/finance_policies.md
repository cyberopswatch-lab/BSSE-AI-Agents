# BSSE Finance Policies

## Expense Reimbursement
- Submit within 30 days; receipts required for items over $25
- Travel: book 14 days in advance; economy class for domestic under 6 hours

## Budget Authority
- Under $5,000: Director approval
- $5,000–$25,000: CEO approval
- Over $25,000: Board notification required

## Invoicing
- Client invoices: Net-30; late fee 1.5%/month after 45 days
- All contracts require signed SOW before work begins

## Fiscal Year
- January 1 – December 31; budget planning starts October 1
