# BSSE HR Policies

## Hiring
- All candidates must complete background check and security pre-screening
- Cleared positions require TS/SCI minimum
- Interview process: screening call → technical assessment → panel interview → offer

## PTO
- 20 days PTO annually, accrued monthly
- 11 federal holidays
- Unused PTO rolls over up to 10 days

## Compensation Reviews
- Annual performance reviews in Q4
- Merit increases effective January 1
- Bonus pool: up to 15% of base, distributed Q1

## Remote Work
- Remote-first; all roles remote unless client site requires presence
- Home office stipend: $1,000 one-time, $75/month ongoing

## Onboarding
- 30-day structured onboarding program
- Security awareness training required Week 1
- Clearance sponsorship available for eligible hires
