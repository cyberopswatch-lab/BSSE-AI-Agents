Premium national security consulting. Key differentiators: founder credibility (29yr USAF/USCYBERCOM/NSA), cleared workforce, operational perspective.

## Growth Strategy FY2026-2028
- Year 1: 3 anchor government clients; brand via speaking/publishing
- Year 2: Add commercial practice; grow to 15 staff

## Key Risks
1. Single-founder dependency — mitigate via early senior hire
2. Clearance delays — maintain cleared candidate pipeline
3. DoD budget uncertainty — diversify across agencies and commercial
4. AI commoditizing advisory — differentiate on execution
